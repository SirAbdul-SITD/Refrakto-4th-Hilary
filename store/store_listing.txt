Refrakto: Laser Align Puzzle

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):

FULL DESCRIPTION:
🔆 AIM. REFLECT. ALIGN. 🔆

Each emitter fires a colored laser. Rotate it to point in the right direction so its beam — bouncing off mirrors, blocked by walls — lands on the target of the same color. Light up every target to win.

🎯 HOW TO PLAY
• Tap an emitter to rotate its beam (up → right → down → left)
• Beams reflect off angled mirrors and stop at walls
• Match each colored beam to its same-colored target
• Light every target to complete the level

✨ FEATURES
• 150 levels: 5×5, 6×6 and 7×7 grids with 1–3 beams
• Every puzzle is guaranteed solvable — built from a real beam layout
• Live beam tracing updates the instant you rotate
• Mirrors and walls add bounce-and-block routing
• Sleek neon visuals and an immersive ambient soundtrack
• Haptic feedback
• 100% offline, no ads, no purchases, zero data collection

🧠 PURE OPTICAL DEDUCTION
With multiple beams crossing one grid, lighting one target can block another. Read the bounces, plan the angles, and snap every laser home.

Aim the light. Align them all. 🔆
